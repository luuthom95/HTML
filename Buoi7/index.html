About me

I'm a creative web designer & developer based in Warwickshire, UK. I love crafting, dynamic, engaging websites using the lastest browser technologies, as well as designing for alternative platform such as mobile devices and Facebook.


I've worked in conjunction with some of biggest brands name in the UK, including Next,Very,Play.com,Sky,TheAA, and more.
